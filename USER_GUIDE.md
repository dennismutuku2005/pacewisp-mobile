# PaceWisp Enterprise User Manual
Version: 1.0.0

Welcome to **PaceWisp Enterprise**, the ultimate management suite for Wireless Internet Service Providers. This document provides a comprehensive walkthrough of the application's features, security, and setup.

---

## 1. Getting Started: Authentication & Entry

PaceWisp uses a secure, subdomain-based authentication system to connect you to your specific cloud instance.

### Step 1: Subdomain Setup
When you first open the app, you will be prompted to enter your **Subdomain**. 
- **Example**: If your portal is `http://internet-mtaani.pacewisp.co.ke`, your subdomain is `internet-mtaani`.
- The app will automatically probe the URL to ensure the server is online.

### Step 2: Signing In
Enter your provided **Username** and **Password**. 
- **Pro Tip**: Use the "eye" icon to toggle password visibility.
- Login session persistence ensures you stay logged in even after closing the app, unless you manually log out.

---

## 2. Secure App Lock (Biometric Security)

PaceWisp features enterprise-grade security to protect your financial data and customer logs.

### Enabling the Lock
1. Navigate to **Settings** (Gear icon on the sidebar).
2. Look for **ENABLE BIOMETRIC LOCK**.
3. Toggle the switch to "On".
4. You will be prompted to authenticate with your system's biometric (Fingerprint or Face ID).

### How it Works
Once enabled, every time you minimize the app or lock your screen, PaceWisp will require a biometric scan to re-open. This ensures that unauthorized users cannot access your dashboard even if they have your phone.

## 3. The Dashboard (Command Center)

The main screen provides a high-level overview of your business health.

- **Real-Time Widgets**: View "Today's Earnings", "Active Users", and "Monthly Income".
- **Dynamic Charts**: Interactive line and bar charts visualize your revenue trends over the last 7 days.
- **Router Status**: A live feed of your network nodes. Green indicates a healthy connection; red indicates a node that requires attention.
- **Global Search (Ctrl+K)**: Quickly find customers, vouchers, or specific transaction logs using the search bar at the top/sidebar.

## 4. Module Walkthrough

### 🎫 Vouchers Page
Manage your bandwidth currency.
- **Generate**: Create single or bulk vouchers for different speeds.
- **Track**: See which vouchers are active and which have been used.
- **Cleanup**: Delete expired or unused vouchers to keep your database lean.

### 👥 Customers & History
- **Search**: Enter a phone number or name to find a customer.
- **Session History**: Tap a customer to see their entire history of connections, payments, and data usage.

### 💰 Finance & Reports
- **Income Logs**: A detailed breakdown of every cent coming into the system.
- **Entries**: Monitor live connection attempts and successful logins in real-time.

## 5. Home Screen Widgets (Glanceable Monitoring)

Monitor your earnings without even opening the app.

### How to Install
1. Long-press an empty space on your phone's home screen.
2. Select **Widgets**.
3. Find **PaceWisp** and drag the widget to your screen.

### Features & Security
- **Secure Default (Privacy)**: The widget starts in a **Blurred State**, showing `KSH ***`. This prevents strangers from seeing your income when you're just using your phone in public.
- **Tap to Reveal**: Tap the widget to instantly un-blur and fetch **Live Data** directly from the server.
- **Multiple Accounts**: If you manage multiple WISPs, you can go to **Settings > Home Widget Configuration** and select which specific account should be displayed on the widget.
